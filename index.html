    Make an Impact with a Degree in Entrepreneuship, Technology and More




    Our Degree in Sustainability, Entrepreneuship and Technology will help you build the competencies needed to become a founder or further develop your Entrepreneurial skills to put your ideas into action in a corporate enviroment. We want to help you join an existing venture or create one of your own.

    A More Sustainable Future Starts with you

    Grow and learn with the best. Your new peers, world-renowned mentors, lecturers from successful startups, corporates, and venture capital firms will all join your network.

    Develop the skills, mindset, and competencies needed to create a more sustainable future. Whether you have an idea for a new type of technology or a sustainable business model, we're here to help you make it a reality.

    Gain credibility with a Master of Science (CE) in SET, issued by the WU Executive Academy which has over 100 years of experience and is credited as one of the leading business universities worldwide.

    The Future Is Yours

    The Master (CE) in Sustainability, Entrepreneurship, and Technology helps you learn, apply, and master the skills needed to create a better and more sustainable tomorrow.

    ToU offers a mobile-first learning platform designed for and by our students. Our challenge-based and interactive learning format will enable you to test your ideas and develop competencies in the areas needed in the 21st-century.
